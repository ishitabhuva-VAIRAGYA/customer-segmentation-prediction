# Customer Segment Profiles

## 1. Premium Spenders

* High annual income
* High spending score
* Low churn risk
* Best target for loyalty rewards

## 2. Budget Conscious

* Moderate income
* Low spending score
* Medium churn risk
* Sensitive to discounts

## 3. Young Professionals

* Younger age group
* Medium-high spending
* Responsive to personalized offers
* High digital engagement
